Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 12, 2025

InfiniteBot is a Discord bot that offers AI-powered conversations, moderation features, and interactive utilities. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, and use your data. By using the bot, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Discord IDs (user, channel, guild IDs) for feature functionality.
  • Message content when you directly interact with the AI or use moderation commands.
  • Server settings like enabled features, banned users, and QOTD configurations.
  • Logs for moderation events, temporarily stored for troubleshooting.

2. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services which may collect and process your data:

  • Google Cloud / Gemini API – Processes AI chat requests, which may include message content.
  • Discord – All bot interactions happen through Discord's API, which has its own privacy policy.

3. How We Use Data

Data is used solely to provide bot features, including AI responses, moderation actions, and server-specific customizations. We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.

4. Data Storage & Security

We store necessary data in secure databases. Sensitive data such as messages sent to AI services are not permanently stored by us, but may be temporarily processed by third-party APIs.

5. Your Rights

Server admins can request deletion of server-related data at any time by contacting the bot owner via Discord. Individual users may request removal from AI interaction lists.
